Radu Andrei Otopeleanu received his B. Sc. degree in Technologies and Systems of Telecommunications in 2018 and his Master of Science in Mobile Communications in 2024 from the Faculty of Electronics, Telecommunications and Information Technology, Politehnica University of Bucharest, Romania. He is currently pursuing his PhD. thesis regarding adaptive filtering techniques at the Doctoral School of Electronics, Telecommunications & Information Technology, National University of Science and Technology POLITEHNICA Bucharest. He is also a Teaching Assitant at the same faculty where his main teaching activities involve leading laboratory sessions for the "Signals and Systems", "Digital Signal Processing" and "Radar" courses.

Programming/configuration and testing technologies: Python 3.x (NumPy, TensorFlow, Pandas, PyTorch), Matlab, Simulink.

Laboratories: Signals and Systems, Digital Signal Processing, Radar.
